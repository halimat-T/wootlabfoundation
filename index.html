About Wootlab foundation

Wootlab foundation is an organization that is set up with the goal of providing education to the less privileged in the society, and our mission is to strengthen education through advocacy, Investment of resources, and programs that empower every student to achieve both academic and personal success.... The organization depends on funding from donors to carry out its programs and activities.

What we offer

Wootlab foundation is working to move the needle and help every student consistently achieve positive academic and personal growth. We are dedicated to ensuring that all students have equitable opportunities to benefit from high quality education so the can graduate prepared for career and life success.

OUR PROGRAM

Our program is an initiative to ensure that less privileged children receive an enrich learning experience by our team. Our teams are present in these schools on an agreed day and time of the week to teach them various subjects.
